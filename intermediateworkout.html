Intermediate Workout

Workout Routine


    3-Day Split (Push / Pull / Legs) – Max 3 Sets

    Day 1 – Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

    Day 2 – Pull (Back, Biceps)

    Day 3 – Legs & Core

    💥 Day 1 – Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
      Barbell Bench Press 3 sets, 6–8 reps
      Dumbbell Shoulder Press 3 sets, 8–10 reps
      ncline Dumbbell Press 3 sets, 8–10 reps
      Lateral Raises 3 sets, 12–15 reps
      Triceps Pushdown (Cable) 3 sets, 10–12 reps

    🔁 Day 2 – Pull (Back, Biceps)
      Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldown 3 sets, 6–10 reps
      Barbell or Dumbbell Rows 3 sets, 8–10 reps
      Seated Cable Row 3 sets, 10–12 reps
      Face Pulls 3 sets, 12–15 reps
      Dumbbell or Barbell Curls 3 sets, 10–12 reps


    🦵 Day 3 – Legs & Core
      Barbell Back Squat or Leg Press 3 sets, 6–8 reps
      Romanian Deadlift (Dumbbell or Barbell) 3 sets, 8–10 reps
      Walking Lunges 3 sets, 12 reps per leg
      Standing or Seated Calf Raises 3 sets, 15–20 reps,
      Hanging Leg Raises or Cable Crunches 3 sets, 12–15 sets
