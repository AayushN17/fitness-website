Beginner Workout

Workout Routine


    Do 2–3 sets of each exercise. Rest for 60–90 seconds between sets.

    1. Leg Press (or Bodyweight Squat)

    10–12 reps

    Focus: Quads, hamstrings, glutes

    2. Lat Pulldown (or Assisted Pull-Up)

    10–12 reps

    Focus: Back, biceps

    3. Chest Press Machine (or Dumbbell Bench Press)

    10 reps

    Focus: Chest, shoulders, triceps

    4. Dumbbell Shoulder Press

    8–10 reps

    Focus: Shoulders, upper arms

    5. Seated Row Machine

    10–12 reps

    Focus: Back, biceps

    6. Plank Hold

    20–30 seconds

    Focus: Core .
